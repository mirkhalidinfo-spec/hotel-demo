Welcome to The Aurum

Where Every
Moment is Precious.

Nestled in the heart of Rampurhat, The Aurum is West Bengal's premier luxury destination. A sanctuary of refined elegance where world-class hospitality meets authentic Bengali warmth.

From our award-winning restaurant to our handcrafted suites, every detail has been curated to create an experience that lingers long after you leave.

Our Story

A Legacy of
Uncompromising Excellence.

Founded in 2009 by the Sharma family, The Aurum was born from a singular vision — to create a luxury experience worthy of the world stage, rooted in the soul of Bengal.

The Aurum began as a 12-room boutique property with a small dining room. Today, it stands as a 48-room luxury hotel with three distinct dining venues — each offering a different facet of the world's finest cuisines.

Our philosophy is simple: every guest deserves to feel like royalty. From the hand-picked thread count of our linens to the sourcing of our ingredients — no detail is too small.

Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Rajeev Banerjee — trained at Taj Hotels and Le Cordon Bleu Paris — crafts menus that celebrate both global technique and Bengal's extraordinary produce.
