Our Story

Redefining Luxury

A legacy built on trust, vision, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Founded in 2024, NOVA Estate emerged from a simple belief: that extraordinary homes deserve extraordinary representation. We set out to create a real estate experience that matches the caliber of the properties we represent.

Our team brings together decades of combined experience in luxury real estate, architecture, interior design, and investment strategy. We don't just sell homes — we curate lifestyles, preserve legacies, and connect exceptional people with exceptional places.
